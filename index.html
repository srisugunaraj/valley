Valley of Birds Homestay is a simple Farm House with modern amenities, situated 3500 feet above sea level in the Western Ghats at the foothills of Kemmanagundi, a hill station that was the summer retreat of the former Maharaja of Mysore, HRH Krishnaraja Wodeyar IV. Kemmanagundi is about 250 km from Bangalore.more
